Easy and Tasty Cheese Sandwich

Just put some stuff in between breads and eat isn't it. Well this recipe is no different...

Eat this tasty veg sandwich and forget about all the calories a regular sandwich brings at once.

Ingredients:

for sandwich:

  • Slices of bread(wholewheat, multigrain, standard)
  • fresh vegetables(carrot, capsicum, onion, tomatoes, etc. as per your choice)
  • butter(unsalted)
  • a slice of cheese

for chilli sauce:

  • 2 cups of coriander with stem removed
  • 9-10 medium size green chillies
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic
  • some bread crumbs to retain water

Method:

  1. drind the ingredients for the chilli sauce in a mixer for 2 min. or until mixed smoothly
  2. Next, prepare the sandwich by applying butter on a bread
  3. Now, apply the chilli sauce over the butter
  4. Finely chop the vegetables and add them in a bowl together with some salt and mixed spices of your choice
  5. put this mixture above the chilli sauce we applied earlier on the bread
  6. Next, put a slice of cheese on the mixture roughly covering it
  7. Finish by putting another piece of bread on top

  8. Note:you can toast the sandwich in pan at this stage, start by putting some oil/butter on the pan. Then put the sandwich on it. Cook until golden on one side. Flip and cook unitll done.

  9. Serve by sprinkling some parsley over it as garnish
